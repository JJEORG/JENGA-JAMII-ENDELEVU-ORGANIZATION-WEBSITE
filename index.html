About Us

Jenga Jamii Endelevu Organization (JJE) is a non-governmental organization based in Tanzania, focusing on community empowerment through education, advocacy, and sustainable development initiatives.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision:

A thriving and inclusive community where every individual is empowered with the resources, opportunities, and support needed to realize their full potential.

Mission:

  • Alleviate poverty
  • Promote gender equality
  • Ensure access to quality healthcare
  • Advance environmental conservation and sustainable development
  • Provide legal aid to the community
